Oil Pipeline Accidents and its Aftereffects

  • Dashboard

The project aims to visualize "Oil pipeline leaks and their aftereffects." Oil pipeline accidents are hazardous and can result in significant economic burdens, making it important to understand their causes to prevent recurring trends in the future. The project aims to identify patterns and trends in spills, determine which areas are most prone to spills, which types of oil are most frequently spilled, and what the causes are. The project also focuses on the aftermath of the accidents by analyzing the damage cost incurred by these spills. The visualizations presented by this project will help policymakers determine appropriate levels of oversight and regulation to prevent spills and mitigate their impacts.
